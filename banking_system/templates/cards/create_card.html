Card Types Comparison

Virtual Debit Card

  • Linked directly to account balance
  • Spend only up to funded amount
  • Ideal for everyday purchases
  • Lower risk for regular transactions

Virtual Credit Card

  • Manual funding from account
  • Set specific amounts per purpose
  • Better for subscription services
  • Ideal for recurring payments

Security Information

Virtual cards add an extra layer of security by isolating your main account from online merchants. If a virtual card is compromised, you can simply deactivate it without affecting your main account.
