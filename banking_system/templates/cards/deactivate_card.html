About Card Deactivation
What happens when you deactivate a card?
  • The card will be immediately marked as inactive
  • All future transactions attempted with this card will be declined
  • Any recurring payments set up with this card will fail
  • The card will still appear in your list of cards, but marked as inactive
  • Remaining balance will still be accessible for withdrawal to your account
Tip: If you're deactivating due to security concerns, you may want to create a new card for future use.
